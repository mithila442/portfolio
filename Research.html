STR-VQA: Saliency-Driven Transfer Learning with Register Tokens for Spatiotemporal VQA

  • Keywords: Video Quality Assessment, Register Tokens, Saliency Modeling, UNet3D, Transformer
  • Dr. Mylene Farias, Mayesha Maliha Rahman Mithila
  • February 2024
  • Description: STR-VQA is a no-reference video quality assessment model that combines saliency prediction and register-token-based memory encoding to improve perceptual alignment. It integrates a UNet3D-based saliency module with a ResNet + Transformer architecture, achieving state-of-the-art performance across KonVid-1k, LIVE-VQC, and YouTube-UGC.
